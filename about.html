How it works

Through our unique formulation of carefully-selected active ingredients with strong biocidal properties, Viridis is able to kill 99.999% of bacteria, viruses, yeasts and moulds after application. RBT enables Viridis to continue to inactivate pathogens long after it dries. After application, Viridis crystalises and forms a protective barrier over surfaces. This invisible shield, which can repel and kill pathogens, can remain intact for up to 24 hours in mild abrasion environments.
